Ingredients

Steps

  1. Heat up your coals and put it on the grill
  2. Grill the chicken low and slow for 15 minutes until desired internal temperature is reached
  3. After hitting the correct internal temperature, sear the chicken on a higher heat to have a beautiful outside crust
