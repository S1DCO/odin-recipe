Lasagna

This is a picture of a lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna sheet on a pot of boiling water for 10 minutes
  2. Saute the aromatics then add the beef
  3. Pour the tomato sauce when the beef is cooked
  4. Alternate a layer of sauce and the lasagna sheets on a baking pan
  5. Cook the lasagna on an oven for how long you like
